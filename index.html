How I work in 2026

Modern SEO is no longer just ranking pages. It is about building a discovery system that works across search engines, answer engines and AI interfaces. This section shows that process in a cleaner and more visual way.

01. Understand the intent landscape

I start by mapping how buyers search, compare and ask questions across traditional search, communities and AI tools. This helps identify the right content angle, funnel stage and query format before execution begins.

Query mapping Audience signals SERP analysis AI prompt behaviour

02. Build structured content systems

Instead of isolated posts, I create connected topic systems with clear hierarchy, internal linking and content depth. That makes pages easier to crawl, easier to trust and easier to surface in both search and answer-led results.

Topic clusters Internal linking E-E-A-T signals Content design

03. Improve technical clarity

I make sure search engines and AI systems can understand the site properly by fixing crawl paths, indexation issues, content structure and page-level relevance signals.

Robots.txt Sitemaps Schema Site architecture

04. Turn visibility into acquisition

The final step is making sure visibility leads somewhere meaningful. I connect traffic to conversions through better page journeys, stronger middle-of-funnel content and sharper alignment between search intent and business goals.
